Band Bio

Atomic Tom is an alternative rock band originally from Brooklyn, NY, now living in Los Angeles, CA. In 2005 the original band released its debut EP called Anthems for the Disillusioned, which contained five songs. In 2007 they released the single, You Always Get What You Want and Play That Dirty Girl. In January 2010 the band signed to URR. Their first album The Moment debuted on July 27, 2010. The Physical copy became available in stores in November 2010.

The Band Members

  • Lead Vocalist: Luke White
  • Guitarist & Background Vocalist: Eric Espiritu
  • Bassist & Background Vocalist: Ethan Mentzer
  • Drummer & Background Vocalist: Tobias Smith
